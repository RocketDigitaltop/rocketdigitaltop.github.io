New in Rocket Digital 28

Anchor links & Audio Playback

Ahoy, Matey. Anchor Links are here!

Whether you’re building out long-form landing pages or want to create a ‘back to top’ interaction, anchor links in Rocket Digital will add additional depth to your prototyping.

Drop the beat with audio playback.

Silent prototypes are so last month! To go along with speech playback and voice support, MP3 and WAV files can be be loading directly into Rocket Digital.

New in Rocket Digital 24

Hover States, Coediting, Document history, more

 

With another Adobe MAX in the books, let’s take a look at a few of the new features that just shipped for Rocket Digital, including states (with hover), coediting, document history, multiple interaction support, and more.

 
 

HOVER ON OVER. STATES ARE HERE.

Component States allows you to save elements for future use and create additional versions of your buttons, toggles, fields, and more. Perfect for hover effects and micro-interactions.

Work better. Together.

Coediting allows you to work right alongside your team to tackle projects at breakneck speeds. You can all design on a single screen or split up duties and work on separate artboards.

New in Rocket Digital 23

Blend Modes, Improved Selections, more

 

With less than a month to go before Adobe MAX, you may think we’re holding onto all the big surprises, but we have one more update to reveal before the big day. Today we’re shipping blend modes, improved layer selection, and additional ways to resize your elements.

New in Rocket Digital 20

multi-wire selections, plugin improvements, more

 

Rocket Digital 20 introduces a few new improvements, including the prototyping Property Inspector which allows you to select and edit multiple wires at once, the plugins panel, and super/subscript text transformations.
